learn to crochet

It's really easy to get started! To begin your first project, you only need to learn a couple key stitches and get a few basic supplies. The supplies you'll need are generally quite affordable and easy to source - you can learn more about sourcing supplies here.

For any and all crochet projects, you'll need:

Yarn

A crochet hook

A darning needle

Scissors

Depending on what you're making, you might also need:

Stitch markers

Safety eyes

Stuffing

Buttons

There are a few key techniques you should start with. With just these 4 skills, you can make a huge range of items including amigurumis, garments, bags, blankets, and more. Click on the images for tutorials on each skill.
